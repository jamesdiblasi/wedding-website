Under the Tuscan sun

We’re finally tying the knot! Journey to the Italian countryside and join us as we get married overlooking the Tuscan hills. As you all know, Italy is one of our steadfast favourite places to visit and we want you by our sides to finally tie the knot. Raise a glass of prosecco by the pool before the ceremony and get stuck into a feast afterwards over a few chianti classicos. Surrounded by beautiful olive trees, lemons, oranges and grapefruit, lavender and white roses, we have the bright and colourful 16th century L’Arancera di Villa Grabau all to ourselves for our destination wedding. The reception will be held here also, so dance the night away with us and don’t worry about driving home, we’ll have transport ready to take you back to anywhere you’re staying in Lucca. The Wedding is on the 6th of June 2023.
